NEWCASTLE EXHIBITION AND CONVENTION CENTRE – AN EVENTS AND ENTERTAINMENT DESTINATION IN THE HEART OF A DYNAMIC HARBOUR CITY.

NEX is currently being developed into an unrivalled exhibition, convention and entertainment destination for world-class events. Contemporary design, flexible spaces and cutting-edge technology will combine to make NEX the obvious choice for touring superstars, event organisers, corporate or wedding planners.

CONVENTIONS
& EXHIBITION

– One of the largest indoor function venues in the Hunter, NEX can be configured for groups of any size (up to 2,400). Large concerts, grand exhibitions or intimate wedding and dinner functions can all be perfectly accommodated by our integrated and flexible spaces. Auditoriums feature a stage, dance floor, bar areas, artists rooms and the latest in entertainment technology. More intimate functions rooms can be configured with private bars, portable dance floors, breakout rooms and superior technology.

Master chef Hudson Daley has developed a range of impressive functions menus featuring the finest in fresh, seasonal produce catering for all dietary requirements.

NEX offers first class service in a resort style atmosphere at the Executive Inn and the Gateway Inn Newcastle. We have also partnered with accommodation providers within walking distance to NEX. Package options available.

WEDDINGS

Our versatile function rooms can host a wedding of grand proportions or a small intimate, sophisticated soiree. The NEX expert team of dedicated wedding and event planners will take care of every fine detail, and can provide styling to suit your chosen theme. Master chef Hudson Daley will personally oversee the preparation of a customised wedding menu featuring the freshest seasonal produce and all of your favourite fare.

ABOUT
NEWCASTLE

Newcastle is a cosmopolitan harbour city located just 2 hours north of Sydney, right on the doorstep of the beautiful vineyards of the Hunter Valley. Here you’ll find spectacular beaches, hip bars, quirky boutiques, world-class restaurants and a booming arts scene. And that’s not all. As Australia’s second oldest city, there’s plenty of fascinating historical sites and stories to discover, and surprises around every corner.
